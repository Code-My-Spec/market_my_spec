marketing operations harness

A marketing harness for founders running their own GTM.

Same rigor as the coding sibling. Different audience. The brand lives where attention is the medium — channels, levers, loops — and every surface is wired to one re-skinnable token block.

Roles

03 · operator surfaces

Six surfaces, mapped to badge variants. The mapping is theme-driven — change a token, every role reweights in lockstep.

research

Read the room before you write to it. Audience, market, competitor truth.

strategy

Pick the lever that moves the loop. Refuse the levers that do not.

copy

Say the thing in the fewest accurate words. Cut the rest.

content

Ship the artifact. Pages, posts, sequences — durable, not disposable.

cro

Tighten the funnel from the inside out. Measure before you decorate.

channels

Place the work where the audience already is. Pay for reach honestly.
